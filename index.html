When a standard resume isn't enough

Hey and welcome! My name is Alexander Clines and the purpose of this website is to go beyond what a resume can accomplish. Here you can find more about me than you ever could on a one page resume. In the Resume Section you will find a more comprehensive resume, containing all those things that you might want to know about me but could not find a home on my standard resume. In addition, you will also be able to visit my Projects Section where you can see my work. It's one thing to say I know a language, it's another to show that I know a language and with this website, I aim to do both.


Why isn't there a lot here?

Yes, this website is pretty sparse. That can be attributed to two things.

First and foremost, in case you haven't seen my resume yet, web design is not on there. That being said I am currently self-learning it now and this website is more or less the gym at which I am training my website design skills. So check back in every now and then and you will see the progression in my training.

Secondly, I am still in college, so I haven't been around that long. On top of that, as you probably remember, college is pretty time consuming. Between the various classes, studying, doing homework, and being involved in organizations, I keep pretty busy. On top of that, I spend a lot of time helping others with their work and projects as well. So, while I would love to spend all day working on various projects of my own, I must uphold a certain rule that I was initially forced to obey by my mother, and then later on came to accept as my way of life. A rule that has gotten me this far, and I expect to get me farther. That rule is academics first
